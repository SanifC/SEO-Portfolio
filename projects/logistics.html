UK Based Logistics Website

SEO Case Study & Implementation

Project Overview

A UK-based logistics company needed to improve their online visibility and generate more organic leads. The main challenge was competing in a highly saturated market while targeting specific geographic regions.

SEO Strategy & Implementation

  • Conducted comprehensive keyword research focusing on logistics services in specific UK regions
  • Optimized website structure and navigation for better user experience
  • Created location-specific service pages with targeted content
  • Implemented local SEO best practices including Google Business Profile optimization
  • Built high-quality backlinks from relevant industry directories and publications

Technical Improvements

  • Enhanced website loading speed through image optimization and caching
  • Implemented schema markup for services and locations
  • Fixed crawling issues and improved internal linking structure
  • Made the website mobile-responsive with AMP implementation

Results

Within 6 months of implementing our SEO strategy, the website achieved:

  • 85% increase in organic search traffic
  • Top 3 rankings for primary regional logistics keywords
  • 60% increase in qualified lead generation
  • Improved website authority score from 15 to 45
  • Enhanced local visibility across target regions
