Frequently Asked Questions

What is PPC advertising?

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is a digital marketing model where advertisers pay only when users click on their ads. It's an effective way to drive targeted traffic to your website and generate immediate results.

How long does it take to see results?

PPC campaigns can generate immediate traffic, but optimizing for best performance typically takes 2-3 months. We continuously monitor and adjust campaigns to improve ROI over time.

What's a good budget to start with?

Starting budgets vary by industry and goals, but we recommend a minimum of $1000/month for effective testing and optimization. We'll help you determine the optimal budget based on your objectives.

How do you measure success?

We track key metrics including click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, cost per conversion, and return on ad spend (ROAS). We provide regular reports showing these metrics and their impact on your business goals.
